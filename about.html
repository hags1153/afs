Who We Are

Atlantis Fire and Security has proudly served the Greater Boston area and all of Eastern Massachusetts for over two decades. As a local, independently owned business, we specialize in delivering tailored fire alarm and security solutions that larger companies simply can’t match.

What sets us apart isn’t just our cutting-edge technology or licensed, highly trained technicians—it’s our people. We care deeply about our customers’ safety, and we treat every home and business like it’s our own.

Locally Rooted. Regionally Trusted.

From Boston to Cape Cod, we’ve built a reputation for timely, dependable service and custom security solutions. Whether it’s a residential smoke detector or a full-scale commercial fire alarm system, Atlantis delivers quality that’s tough to find anywhere else.
