Who We Are

Atlantis Fire and Security has proudly served the Greater Boston area and all of eastern/central Massachusetts, and southern New Hampshire for over two decades. As a local, independently owned business, we specialize in delivering tailored fire alarm and security solutions that larger companies simply can’t match.

What sets us apart isn’t just our willingness to do good by you. Our licensed technicians are experienced, and we are a tight-knit group with one goal; Our Customers Safety and Security. We treat every home and business like it’s our own.

Locally Rooted. Regionally Trusted.

From Nashua to Cape Cod, we’ve are focused on building a reputation for around timely, dependable service and custom security solutions. Whether it’s a residential smoke detector or a full-scale commercial fire alarm system, Atlantis delivers quality that’s tough to find anywhere else.
